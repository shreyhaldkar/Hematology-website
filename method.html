Material & Method

Step 1: Blood Collection

Blood was collected from Labeo rohita using the caudal vessel and cardiac puncture methods. Fish were first anesthetized before collection to minimize stress.

  • Caudal Vessel Puncture: The fish was placed ventral side up, and a fine needle (22‚Äì26 G) attached to a 5 mL syringe was inserted along the mid-ventral line just posterior to the anal fin until it contacted the spinal column. Gentle suction was applied until blood entered the syringe.
  • Cardiac Puncture: The fish was anesthetized and held ventral side up. The syringe, pre-flushed with heparin solution, was inserted vertically between the anterior bases of the pectoral fins to access the heart. Gentle suction was applied until blood filled the syringe.

The collected blood was transferred to chilled, labelled EDTA tubes (1.5‚Äì2 mL) and gently mixed by inversion to prevent clotting.

Step 2: Blood Smear Preparation

A clean, grease-free glass slide was used for preparing the blood smear.

  • A drop of blood was placed about 2 cm from one end of the slide.
  • Another slide was held at a 45¬∞ angle and its edge touched to the drop. The spreader slide was swiftly pushed forward to spread the blood into a thin, feathered film.
  • The smear was air-dried naturally at room temperature without blowing or heating.

Step 3: Fixation

The prepared smears were fixed using absolute methanol to preserve cellular morphology.

  • The slides were placed vertically in a coupling jar filled with absolute methanol until the smear area was covered.
  • The slides were left for 2‚Äì5 minutes and then air-dried upright on absorbent paper.
Fig 4. Fixation of blood smear slides using methanol

Step 4: Staining Techniques

Leishman and Giemsa stains were used for differential staining of the blood cells.

  • Each slide was covered with 1 mL of Leishman stain for 1‚Äì2 minutes.
  • Without removing the Leishman stain, an equal volume of Giemsa working solution was added and mixed gently.
  • A metallic sheen appearing on the surface indicated proper stain interaction.
  • After 14‚Äì20 minutes, slides were rinsed with distilled water and air-dried vertically.
