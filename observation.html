The erythrocytes of Labeo rohita were oval, nucleated red blood cells with centrally located nuclei and haemoglobin-rich cytoplasm. The uniform size and shape of these erythrocytes indicate healthy oxygen-carrying efficiency and good blood quality. Monocyte (WBC)

Monocytes were identified as large, round to oval leukocytes with kidney-shaped nuclei and gray-blue cytoplasm. Their presence in elevated numbers suggests an active immune response, often due to infection or inflammation. In this study, the average monocyte count per field was about 2 cells.
